Detailed Description

Represents a trajectory consisting of multiple poses.

The Trajectory class provides functionality to manage and manipulate a series of poses that form a trajectory. It includes methods for calculating curvature, interpolating between key frames, merging trajectories, and validating the trajectory based on distance and curvature thresholds.
