\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a trajectory consisting of multiple poses. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classikg_1_1_trajectory}{Trajectory}} class provides functionality to manage and manipulate a series of poses that form a trajectory. It includes methods for calculating curvature, interpolating between key frames, merging trajectories, and validating the trajectory based on distance and curvature thresholds.
